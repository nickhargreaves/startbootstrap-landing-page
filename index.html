Features

Assignments

CitizenReporter allows news rooms to create location based assignments for their journalists and to citizens.

Secure reporting

By encrypting captured media securing the communication to the servers, reporters need not worry about their content falling into the wrong hands.

Lessons

Having a camera enabled phone is not enough to create awesome content. CitizenReporter will help you polish your journalism skills using the inbuilt lessons functionality.

Payments

CitizenReporter allows freelance journalists to be paid for their content and track their payment history.

Dashboard

CitizenReporter also includes an editorial dashboard that is built on top of the familiar WordPress content management interface.
With it you can:
  • Receive and manage content submitted through the app
  • View and communicate to the journalists
  • Create and manage assignments
  • Publish lessons
